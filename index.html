Color Shift Runner 3D

A fast-paced neon runner where you must match colors to survive. Tap to change colors and pass through obstacles. The speed increases over time.

How to Play

Tap the screen to change the ball color. Match the obstacle color to pass. Wrong color ends the game.

Features

Endless gameplay • Combo system • Leaderboard • Unlockable skins

Challenge yourself and try to reach the top score!
